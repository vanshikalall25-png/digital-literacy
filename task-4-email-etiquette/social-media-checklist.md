# Social Media Do’s & Don’ts

## Do’s
- Keep your profiles professional  
- Use strong and secure passwords  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Respect others online  
- Maintain proper privacy settings  

---

##  Don’ts
- Don’t share personal information publicly  
- Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content  
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying  
- Don’t trust unknown messages blindly
