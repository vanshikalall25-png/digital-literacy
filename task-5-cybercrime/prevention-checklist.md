# Prevention Checklist

##  Cyber Safety Tips
- Never share OTP or PIN with anyone  
- Verify payment requests before approving  
- Avoid clicking unknown or suspicious links  
- Use only official and trusted apps  
- Enable transaction alerts for your bank account  
- Check website URLs carefully before entering details  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Keep your apps and devices updated  
- Use secure internet connections (avoid public Wi-Fi for transactions)  

---

##Report Cybercrime
If you face any cyber fraud or suspicious activity:

- Visit: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call: **1930 (24x7 Helpline)**
