**UPI Fraud**
--

UPI fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India. It usually happens when scammers trick users into sharing sensitive information like OTPs or clicking malicious links. A typical scenario involves a fraudster calling the victim pretending to be a bank representative. They claim there is an issue with the account and ask the user to click a link or approve a payment request.

Once the victim follows the instructions, money gets deducted from their account without their knowledge. Students and elderly people are common targets due to lack of awareness. The consequences include financial loss, stress, and sometimes identity theft.

In many reported cases, victims lose large amounts within minutes. Since transactions are instant, recovery becomes difficult. Awareness and caution are the only ways to prevent such frauds.
